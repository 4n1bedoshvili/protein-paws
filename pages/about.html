OUR JOURNEY

Nestled in the verdant valleys of the Pacific Northwest, NaturaPet Foods was born from a simple yet profound mission: to connect pets with the power of nature. Our founder, Dr. Amelia Hawthorne, a veterinarian with over two decades of experience, noticed a recurring theme in her practice. Many pets suffered from ailments that could be traced back to poor nutrition. Determined to make a difference, Dr. Hawthorne embarked on a journey to create a pet food brand that prioritizes health, sustainability, and the innate wisdom of nature. In 2015, with the support of local farmers, nutritionists, and fellow animal lovers, NaturaPet Foods introduced its first batch of holistic pet food. What started in a small kitchen has grown into a beloved brand, committed to improving the lives of pets through nutrition that mirrors their ancestral diet

OUR COMMITMENT TO SUSTAINABILITY

Understanding our responsibility to the planet, NaturaPet Foods is dedicated to sustainable practices. We source our ingredients from local, ethical farms that share our commitment to animal welfare and environmental stewardship. Our packaging is made from recycled materials and is fully biodegradable, reflecting our pledge to minimize our ecological footprint.
